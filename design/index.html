Capitol Theatre Design

As a part of the Heightened Multi-sensory Experiences class, there was an opportunity to design an experience in the RMIT Capitol Theatre. The project was made to enhance a competitive viewing experience, and there were two intentions behind this. The first was to provide additional context to those unfamiliar with the game to be more aware of who was in the lead and to make the experience more exciting and engaging to all viewers making full use of the RGB theatre.
